pac-man lotto ticket

A mock-up of a Pac-man lotto scratch-off, done with both Photoshop and Illustrator. Created the pac-man, logo, fruits, and ghost in Illustrator before transferring to Photoshop. The most challenging issue with this project was creating the foil and glossy effect. Solved by placing the foil (created in Photoshop) in Illustrator to apply to parts.
