zoo placard

A zoo placard for an African Wild Dog that was done in Illustrator. The most challenging aspect of this project was creating the dog's eyes, creating the iris, and blend of colors. Solved this by researching how to create eyes and used the Roughen and Zig Zag effect along with gradients to achieve the eyes.
